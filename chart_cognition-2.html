Personal, social and emotional development
[ELG01] 发展人际关系
Making relationship
当前发展结果： 继续观察
发展中
与其他人一起玩 Plays alongside others.
使用一个熟悉的成年人作为一个安全的基础，在新环境中独立探索，例如冒险去玩和与他人互动，但如果有焦虑，则返回拥抱或安慰。 Uses a familiar adult as a secure base from which to explore independently in new environments, e.g. ventures away to play and interact with others, but returns for a cuddle or reassurance if becomes anxious.

[ELG02] 自信和自我意识
Self-confidence and Self-awareness
当前发展结果： 发展良好
发展中
探索新的玩具和环境，但是在需要的时候，可以和熟悉的成年人一起定期检查。 Explores new toys and environments, but ‘checks in’ regularly with familiar adult as and when needed.
渐渐地玩假装玩具（支持孩子了解自己的想法可能与别人不同）。 Gradually able to engage in pretend play with toys (supports child to understand their own thinking may be different from others).
表现为个人的自我感觉，例如：想独立做事，对成年人说“不”。 Demonstrates sense of self as an individual, e.g. wants to do things independently, says “No” to adult.
[ELG03] 管理感觉和行为
Managing Feeling and Behaviour
当前发展结果： 发展良好
发展中
知道别人的感觉，例如：如果听到一个熟悉的快乐的声音，听到哭泣或看起来很兴奋的话。 Is aware of others’ feelings, for example, looks concerned if hears crying or looks excited if hears a familiar happy voice.
日益增长的意志和决心可能会导致难以处理的愤怒和沮丧感。可能会发脾气。 Growing sense of will and determination may result in feelings of anger and frustration which are difficult to handle, e.g. may have tantrums.
鼓励和支持响应几个适当的边界。 Responds to a few appropriate boundaries, with encouragement and support.
