Accommodation

Various schools provide different accommodations, from dorms in campus to apartment outside. Normally the school will offer housing subsidy if they don’t have freely dorms in school.
The rent is 2000 to 5000 depending on the quality and place of the flat. So, it is cost-effective if two or three friends or colleagues rent one apartment together and each person one separate bedroom, while sharing kitchen and toilet. We help our teachers settle down, including rent apartment before coming.

Marvelous cuisine

Daily meals: Most schools provide lunch. It takes 5-10 yuan for breakfast and around 20 yuan for dinner. Enjoy food in restaurant: If you go shopping and have fun in weekends, it normally costs you 60-200 yuan for one to three persons.

Recruitment - ESL teachers

Quantities of vacancies:
The vacant capacity is 60 teachers for the new semester (starting from 1st Sept. 2019). We may increase employment based on the qualification of candidates.
Type of work:
  • Kindergarten homeroom teacher: 2.5-6 years old
  • Primary and Secondary school ESL teacher: 6-12 years old in primary school, 12-15 in middle school
  • High school oral English teacher: 15-18 years old
  • After-school and Training school: mainly 3-15 years old. The working time is contrary with normal schools.
  • They work during weekends and evening but have rest during daytime and workdays.
  • We prefer who have arts or sports skills as well as teaching skills. But it is not a must.

Requirements

A B C (Either one of two is ok) Qualified
Native English speaker Bachelor’s degree or above 1. Major in English teaching/ Education A+B+C
2. If not graduated from above majors, TEFOL/TEFL/CELTA certificate is necessary
Notes: We prefer those with more than two years educational work experience. i.e. born no later than 1994 is more preferred.
