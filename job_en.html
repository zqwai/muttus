Specific requirements:

Kindergarten teacher requirements:

  • 1. At least bachelor's degree/diploma in education.
  • 2. Major in Early Year Education (or equivalent majors)
  • 3. Native English speakers.

ESL teacher in primary and secondary school and language training school, requirements:

  • 1. At least bachelor's degree in education.
  • 2. Major in English teaching
  • 3. Native English speakers.

For those who are not major in English or not degree/diploma in education but want to be ESL teacher, requirements:

  • 1. At least bachelor's degree. Degree specific in education/teaching is preferred but not a must.
  • 2. ESL teaching certificate: TEFL/TESOL/CELTA
  • 3. Two years fulltime working experience in teaching.
  • It is not a must for candidates who have Master and PhD degree in education.
  • 4. Native English speakers.

Subjective teacher in primary and secondary schools’ requirements:

  • 1. At least bachelor's degree in arts/music/sports/match or education.
  • 2. Major in painting/drama/music/math and so on
  • 3. Teaching certificate
